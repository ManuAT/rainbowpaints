RAINBOW PAINTING CONTRACTORS

We have started in the year of 1996 in our own building at Ernakulum. Our main business is painting of commercial buildings and residential flats and all other buildings.

After completion of 14 years in the painting contracts for small houses, in January 2006 Rainbow contractors entered into the painting contract business with construction companies like SFS, Puravankara, Mather Builders ,Tritvam, DLF , SKYLINE Builders etc

Now our major client are Skyline builders, TATA, Joyalukkas, NOEL, ASSET, Alive, Chakolas, Yeskay, Favourite, Malabar.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide great quality workmanship without compromise, taking particular care around your projects, always keeping clean and tidy.

VISION

Our vision is to become a respected and regulated painting company that serves the public with our Quality and Standard
